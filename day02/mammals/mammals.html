MAMMALS

This is basic external features of Mammals

  • Females having Mammary Glands is the key feature
  • Exoskeleton made of epidermal outgrowths called Hairs
  • Locomotor structure is mainly Limbs Fore limbs and hind limbs
  • Heart is Four chambered
  • Respiration by Pair of lungs
  • Viviparous i.e fertilization and development inside the body
  • These are Warm blooded animals.
