AMPHIBIANS

This is basic external features of Amphibians

  • There is No exoskeleton
  • Locomotor structure is mainly Fore limbs and Hind Limbs
  • Heart is Three chambered
  • Respiration by Gills
  • Oviparous i.e egg laying animals
  • Development might include a stage called Metamorphosis
  • These are Cold blooded animals.
