REPTILES

This is basic external features of reptiles

  • Exoskeleton is of Epidermal scales
  • Locomotor structure is mainly Fore limbs and Hind Limbs Pentadactyl
  • Heart is Three chambered
  • Respiration by Lungs
  • Oviparous i.e egg laying animals
  • Fertilization internal development external
  • These are Cold blooded animals.
