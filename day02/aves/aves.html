AVES

This is basic external features of Aves

  • Body has exoskeleton made of Feathers
  • Locomotor structure is mainly Limbs Fore limbs are modified into wings
  • Heart is Four chambered
  • Respiration by Pair of lungs
  • Oviparous i.e egg laying animals
  • Fertilisation internal and development is external.
  • These are Warm blooded animals.
