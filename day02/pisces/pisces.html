PISCES

This is basic external features of pisces

  • Body has exoskeleton made of Dermal scales
  • Locomotor structure is mainly Fins
  • Heart is Two chambered
  • Respiration by Gills
  • Oviparous i.e egg laying animals
  • These are Cold blooded animals.
